You Dream, We Chase

EventX aims to revolutionize the event management industry in Pakistan by providing
an online platform for customers and sellers to connect with each other easily. Our goal is to
simplify the event planning process for our customers while providing a profitable business opportunity
for our sellers.

EventX is an online event management system that provides a platform for sellers and customers
to connect with each other. Sellers can showcase their services on our platform while customers
can browse through the system according to their needs. The system includes
marriage halls, catering, guest houses, auditoriums, conference rooms, and
restaurants for birthday parties.
